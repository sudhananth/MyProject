                            <h2 style="text-align:left;">NSS Activities 2014 - 2015</h2>
                            <p>JSS Dental College & Hospital, a constituent college of JSS University conducted NSS camp from 17th to 23rd of February 2014 in Belagola.</p>
                            <p>Dr B Manjunatha, Registrar, JSS University flagged off a week long service program in which Dental, Medical, Ayurvedic checkup and treatment where conducted for about 800 people out of which 250 received the treatments like scaling, filling, extraction,70 echocardiogram and 83 were benefited by ayurvedic treatment. Free medicine were distributed for the patients in need.</p>
                            <p>The people from Belagola and school children were also benefited by speeches in various topics such as women health, food and nutrition, life skills, HIV & AIDS and youth of India by renowned resource person. The schedule also included various activities such as drawing competition, debate, pick and speak, essay writing, street plays and various other extracurricular activities for the government school children.</p>
                            <p>It was Dr B Nandlal, Principal, JSS Dental College and Hospital who stood as a guide force with Dr .Ravi M B, NSS Co-ordinator to accomplish this massive attempt. Dr Sundheendra Bhatt, Deputy Controller (Examination), JSS University graced the valedictory function.</p>
                            <p>The NSS camp was lead by 50 volunteers other doctors from JSS institution. This event lead a strong part to the volunteers for their “LEARING THROUGH EXPERIENCE” and left a strong sense of “NOT ME BUT YOU”- a notion that bears its significant through the NSS movements.</p>
